How It All Fits Together

These aren't isolated tools‚Äîthey're layers of a coherent stack. Holochain provides the agent-centric foundation where each participant maintains sovereignty. hREA and ValueFlows give us the vocabulary to describe economic relationships. Cyclos or Credit Commons handle the actual currency mechanics.

Loomio and Decidim enable democratic governance of these systems. Hylo and IoT sensors connect economic flows to ecological health. IPFS and ThreeFold provide the distributed storage and networking.

The result? Economic systems that automatically redistribute resources based on community health, adapt to changing conditions, and strengthen through cooperative relationships‚Äîjust like mycelial networks in a healthy forest.

Small

300-5,000 Users

5,000+ Users

Full infrastructure:

  • Credit Commons Protocol federation
  • Multi-tier Decidim installations
  • Holochain + hREA integration
  • IoT sensor networks

For regional economies, national cooperatives, and bioregional integration.
